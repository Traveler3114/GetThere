# GetThere MVP Strategy (Very Short Summary)

## Main Idea

- **Don’t start with the full mobility platform.**
- **Start with a simple travel ticket wallet.**

---

## MVP (3–6 months)

Build an app where users can:

- **Store all travel tickets in one place**
- **Import tickets** from:
  - Email
  - PDF
  - QR codes
  - Screenshots
- **See tickets organized into trip timelines**
- **Get departure reminders**

Similar to:

- **Apple Wallet**
- **Google Wallet**

…but **focused only on travel**.

---

## Why This Works

It avoids the hardest problems:

- ❌ No government contracts
- ❌ No transport APIs
- ❌ No operator integrations

But **still solves a real user problem**.

---

## Later Expansion

- **Add ticket purchases inside the app**
- **Integrate transport operators**
- **Add journey planning**

→ Then it becomes a full **Mobility-as-a-Service platform**.

---

## Key Strategy

- **Build users first**, then bring transport companies onto the platform later.
